My course

The Bachelor of Computer Science in Data Engineering at UTM is an 8-semester program designed to produce graduates skilled in managing and processing data efficiently. The course structure integrates theoretical and practical aspects of computer science, emphasizing database systems, information technology, and data management.

Students engage in real-world projects through industrial attachments and hands-on training. The curriculum also aligns with industry needs, ensuring graduates are job-ready and equipped to handle data-centric roles.
